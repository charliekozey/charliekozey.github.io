cosmic pellets

cosmic pellet (noun): a brief interaction, new piece of information, or other small moment that makes life feel meaningful.

This page is where I gather these little things, a somewhat haphazard collection of thoughts and items that I find to be worth remembering and sharing.

2021-02-22 | A moment of calm

How cool is this? A Victoria, BC public art project designed to bring some auditory comfort when you call the toll-free line. Music, poetry, guided meditation, children's laughter. I actually did feel better after a few minutes of listening.

Kathryn Calder, the musican who organized this project, is one of my favorite artists; I love the sincerity and gentle intensity of her music. She's also in the New Pornographers, one of my favorite bands. I had the great pleasure of serendipitously meeting her one April afternoon, and she was so gracious to me, wide-eyed Mary Ann Singleton that I was.

I had walked from work to Kathryn Calder's show at Hotel Utah in San Francisco, and I had arrived early because I planned on getting a quick dinner at the venue. I put in my order and headed back to the small seating area at the end of the bar, and I did a literal double take when I realized she was sitting there at one of the tables. Not running late, not hanging out backstage (although, now that I think about it, maybe there *is* no backstage at Hotel Utah), just sitting there like a normal person, waiting for her own show.

I remember thanking her for her music and probably oversharing about dead mom stuff. At some point she mentioned that she was reading My Brilliant Friend by Elena Ferrante. We discussed El Farolito, a classic SF burrito place, and she said that she misses out on much of the excitement over their burritos since she's a vegetarian. I'm pretty sure that was the moment I decided to revive my own lapsed vegetarianism (though I do miss those burritos sometimes).
