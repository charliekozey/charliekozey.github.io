I'm currently recording demos for Loved Ones EP, a small collection of indie-folky-poppy songs I wrote in 2015-2016 about, well, my loved ones.

I make electronic music as Vabriel and have been known to hang out with the Kaizen Club.

I'm part of Ensemble Consensus, where we explore different ways of relating to one another by listening and sounding together. Our new album song cycling is out now on Infrequent Seams. Check it out on Bandcamp!
