What is this thing?

This is a wiki–a group of interconnected note-like entries–based on ideas I come across as I do background reading and interview-based research for my dissertation. The working title of my project is "Individuality/Intervocality: What Trans Singers Tell Us about Voice and Identity," which may give you some sense of the topics that I will be focusing on.

The notes I include on the wiki will be a kind of middle ground: not the most polished, but not my most nascent, informal musings either. My hope is that by linking all these ideas together in a non-hierarchical way, it will give rise to new insights. I also hope it will be useful to write for a public audience, however small, because it forces me to present nebulous thoughts concisely and clearly.

This little corner of the internet where I play and explore is inspired by Anne-Laure Le Cunff's "digital garden." I love this metaphor because it reminds me of cultivating, tending, pruning, harvesting, and sharing. Not to mention calmness and patience, without which (at least for me) this writing process would be impossible.
