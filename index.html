about charlie kozey

hi!

i'm currently recording demos for loved ones ep, a small collection of songs i wrote in 2015-2016.

i make electronic music as vabriel and have been known to hang out with the kaizen club.

i'm part of ensemble consensus, where we invent new ways of relating to one another by listening and sounding together. our new album song cycling will be out on infrequent seams on november 20, 2020. you can pre-order it and listen to the first track on bandcamp.

finally, i'm writing a dissertation on the relationship between voice and identity. you can explore my thoughts-in-progress here (someday).
